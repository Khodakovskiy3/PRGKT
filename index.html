    about my project “PROGECT”

    My project is designed to show that it doesn't matter how many skills, learning opportunities, or free time you have, but how much you want to do what you love

    skills

    This is an acquired skill, especially in something creative. It is impossible to create something brilliant without understanding what “brilliant” is, because for everyone it is an understanding of personal taste and understanding.

    Learning opportunities
